About Fluency

Fluency was created for adult learners who want practical English skills without hype. Our curriculum focuses on the most useful words and sentence structures, repeated until they become automatic.

We combine short video clips, graded reading passages, and daily speaking drills. Every lesson builds on previous ones, reinforcing vocabulary in context.

No gamification gimmicks – just honest, structured practice. The only thing we track is your consistency, because that's what truly matters.

– Daily video lesson with comprehension check

– Weekly speaking task with peer feedback

– Access to WhatsApp community for questions & practice
